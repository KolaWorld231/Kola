## ğŸ“š Learning & Lessons

### How do I start learning?
1. Go to the Learning Path for your language
2. Click the red highlighted lesson (your current lesson)
3. Complete the exercises
4. Finish the lesson to unlock the next one

### Why are some lessons locked?
Lessons are unlocked in order. Complete the previous lesson to unlock the next one. The first lesson of each unit unlocks when you complete the last lesson of the previous unit.

### How do I unlock locked lessons?
Complete lessons in order:
- Complete the lesson before it
- Complete the last lesson of the previous unit (for first lessons of units)

### Can I skip lessons?
No, lessons must be completed in order to ensure proper learning progression.

### How do I review completed lessons?
Click any green completed lesson on the learning path to review it.

### What happens if I run out of hearts?
If you run out of hearts:
1. **Wait for Regeneration**: Hearts regenerate 1 per 4 hours (up to 5)
2. **Watch an Ad**: Recover hearts by watching an ad (if available)
3. **Purchase Hearts**: Buy hearts from the shop
4. **Wait Until Tomorrow**: Hearts reset daily

### How many hearts do I have?
You start with 5 hearts per day. Hearts regenerate 1 per 4 hours.

### Can I lose hearts?
Yes, you lose 1 heart for each incorrect answer in a lesson.

## ğŸ’ XP, Hearts & Achievements

### What is XP?
XP (Experience Points) measures your learning progress. Earn XP by completing lessons, exercises, and challenges.

### How do I earn XP?
- Complete a lesson: +10-20 XP
- Complete an exercise: +5 XP
- Complete a unit (treasure chest): +50 XP
- Daily challenges: +20-50 XP
- Achievements: Varies

### How do I check my XP?
View your XP on:
- **Dashboard**: See total XP and daily goal progress
- **Profile**: See your XP in your profile
- **Leaderboard**: See XP-based rankings

### What is a streak?
A streak is consecutive days of learning. Complete at least one lesson per day to maintain your streak.

### How do I maintain my streak?
Complete at least one lesson every day. Your streak resets if you miss a day (unless you use a streak freeze).

### What happens if I miss a day?
Your streak resets to 0. Use streak freezes (from the shop) to protect your streak.

### What are achievements?
Achievements are milestones you unlock as you learn:
- Learning streaks
- Total XP milestones
- Lesson completion milestones
- Perfect lessons

## ğŸ“± Technical Issues

### The page won't load. What should I do?
1. **Refresh the page**: Press F5 or Cmd+R / Ctrl+R
2. **Clear cache**: Clear your browser cache
3. **Try a different browser**: Switch to Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
4. **Check internet connection**: Make sure you're connected
5. **Contact support**: If the issue persists

### Lessons won't load. What should I do?
1. **Refresh the page**: Reload the lesson page
2. **Check internet connection**: Make sure you're online
3. **Try a different lesson**: See if the issue persists
4. **Clear browser cache**: Clear cached data
5. **Contact support**: Report the issue

### I'm getting error messages. What should I do?
1. **Read the error message**: It may tell you what's wrong
2. **Try again**: Sometimes temporary errors resolve themselves
3. **Check our Troubleshooting Guide**: See solutions to common issues
4. **Contact support**: If the error persists

### The app is slow. Why?
1. **Check internet connection**: Slow connection can cause slowness
2. **Close other tabs**: Too many tabs can slow your browser
3. **Clear browser cache**: Cached data might be outdated
4. **Try a different browser**: Some browsers are faster
5. **Contact support**: If slowness persists

### Can I use Volo offline?
Currently, Volo requires an internet connection. Offline mode may be available in the future.

## ğŸ“ Learning Questions

### How long does it take to learn a language?
It varies by person and goal:
- **Basic conversation**: Several weeks with daily practice
- **Fluency**: Months to years of consistent practice
- **Daily practice** (30-60 minutes) is key to success

### How often should I practice?
**Recommended**: At least 15-30 minutes daily
- Consistent daily practice is better than long sessions once a week
- Complete your daily goal to stay on track
- Maintain your streak for motivation

### What if I'm stuck on a lesson?
1. **Review the grammar tips**: Click the lightbulb icon ğŸ’¡
2. **Review previous lessons**: Go back and review related content
3. **Practice mode**: Use flashcards to strengthen vocabulary
4. **Try again**: Sometimes you need to see the lesson multiple times

### Can I practice pronunciation?
Currently, pronunciation practice is available through:
- Listening exercises in lessons
- Audio playback in lessons
- Grammar tips with examples

More pronunciation features may be added in the future.
