## ğŸ’ Understanding XP and Hearts

### XP (Experience Points)

**What is XP?**
- XP measures your learning progress
- Earn XP by completing lessons and exercises
- Track your daily XP goal on the dashboard

**How to Earn XP:**
- Complete a lesson: +10-20 XP
- Complete an exercise: +5 XP
- Complete a unit: +50 XP (treasure chest bonus!)
- Daily challenges: +20-50 XP
- Achievements: Varies

### Hearts System

**What are Hearts?**
- Hearts represent your chances to make mistakes
- You start with 5 hearts per day
- Lose 1 heart for each incorrect answer

**Running Out of Hearts:**
- If you run out of hearts, you'll need to:
  1. Wait for hearts to regenerate (1 heart every 4 hours)
  2. Watch an ad to recover hearts (if available)
  3. Purchase hearts from the shop

**Maximum Hearts**: 5
